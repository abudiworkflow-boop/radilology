**Pancreatitis **(plural: **pancreatitides**) refers to [inflammation](/articles/inflammation?lang=us) involving the [pancreas](/articles/pancreas?lang=us). 

It has various forms which can be classified in many, many ways according to time of onset, etiological agent or associated pathology.

- 
[acute pancreatitis](/articles/acute-pancreatitis?lang=us)

[interstitial edematous pancreatitis](/articles/interstitial-oedematous-pancreatitis?lang=us)

- [necrotizing pancreatitis](/articles/necrotising-pancreatitis?lang=us)

- [hemorrhagic pancreatitis](/articles/haemorrhagic-pancreatitis?lang=us)

- [chronic pancreatitis](/articles/chronic-pancreatitis-2?lang=us)

- [gallstone pancreatitis](/articles/gallstone-pancreatitis?lang=us)

- [hereditary pancreatitis](/articles/hereditary-pancreatitis?lang=us)

- [autoimmune pancreatitis](/articles/autoimmune-pancreatitis?lang=us)

- [emphysematous pancreatitis ](/articles/emphysematous-pancreatitis?lang=us)4,5

- [*Ascaris*-induced pancreatitis ](/articles/ascaris-induced-pancreatitis-1?lang=us)1

- [hypertriglyceridemia-induced pancreatitis](/articles/hypertriglyceridaemia-induced-pancreatitis-1?lang=us)

- [tropical pancreatitis](/articles/tropical-pancreatitis?lang=us)

- [pancreatitis associated with cystic fibrosis](/articles/cystic-fibrosis-abdominal-manifestations-1?lang=us)

- 
[segmental pancreatitis](/articles/segmental-pancreatitis?lang=us) 7

​[paraduodenal pancreatitis](/articles/paraduodenal-pancreatitis?lang=us)

Epidemiology, clinical presentation, pathology, radiographic features, therapy and prognosis are discussed in the above articles.

See: [causes of pancreatitis (mnemonic)](/articles/causes-of-pancreatitis-mnemonic?lang=us).
