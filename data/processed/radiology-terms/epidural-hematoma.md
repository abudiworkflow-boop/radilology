# Exercise Science

Last Update: September 10, 2021.

**Definition**

The scientific study of human movement performed to maintain or improve physical fitness.

**Discussion**

Exercise science includes such subjects as biomechanics, exercise physiology, exercise psychology, cardiac rehabilitation, athletic training, and fitness for special population groups.

**Scope and emphasis**

The NLM emphasis is on scholarly works written for the exercise science or other health professional. Scientific studies of yoga, tai chi, and other movement-based systems and practices for health and fitness are of interest. The Library does not collect material on physical education or instructional works for personal fitness.
