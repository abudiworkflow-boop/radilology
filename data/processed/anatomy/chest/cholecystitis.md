**Cholecystitis** refers to any form of inflammation involving the [gallbladder](/articles/gallbladder?lang=us) and has many forms, including:

[acute cholecystitis](/articles/acute-cholecystitis?lang=us)

[acute acalculous cholecystitis](/articles/acute-acalculous-cholecystitis?lang=us)

- [acute calculous cholecystitis](/articles/acute-cholecystitis?lang=us)

- [chronic cholecystitis](/articles/chronic-cholecystitis?lang=us)

- [emphysematous cholecystitis](/articles/emphysematous-cholecystitis?lang=us)

- [suppurative cholecystitis (gallbladder empyema)](/articles/suppurative-cholecystitis?lang=us)

- [xanthogranulomatous cholecystitis](/articles/xanthogranulomatous-cholecystitis?lang=us)

Its epidemiology, clinical presentation, pathology, and radiographic features are dependent on the actual etiology.
